Bamburi Beach Hotel, the excellent service and superior facilities make for an unforgettable stay. Private balconies are featured in all rooms overlooking the beautiful Indian Ocean. Bamburi Beach Hotel guarantees guests a pleasant stay whether in Mombasa for business or pleasure.
